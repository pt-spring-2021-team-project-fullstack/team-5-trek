What is trekking?

Unlike a pleasant hike through the woods for an evening, trekking involves vigorous walking with the specific purposes of exploring and enjoying the scenery. It takes place in wild natual enviroment for multiple days. There are different ways to do a trek and trekking culture often varies from country to country.

Why do it?

Whether you are spending hours on the trails, kayaking, or climbing boulders, trekking will give your whole body a workout, improving your strength, agility and cardio fitness. Being in nature can boost your mood and improve mental health. You'll gain cultural understanding and social benefits. Undergo thrill and addrenaline during wildlife encounters.
